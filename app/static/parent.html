🔒 How Privacy Works

Guardian DNS is designed so that parents never see the specific websites their children visit. Here's what this dashboard shows — and what it hides:

  • Shown: Aggregate blocked counts per category (e.g. "Adult Content: 12")
  • Hidden: The actual domain names, timestamps, and browsing patterns
  • Hidden: Search queries and any content the child accessed
  • Local only: All data stays on your local device — nothing is sent to the cloud
