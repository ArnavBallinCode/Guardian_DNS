AI Risk Scoring

A local LLM (Ollama) evaluates every unknown domain and returns a risk probability from 0.0 to 1.0. No data leaves your machine.

🗳️

Human-in-the-Loop

Adult reviewers vote on AI recommendations. Only when enough humans agree with the AI does a domain get permanently blocked.

🔒

Privacy-First Design

Parents see only category totals, not specific domains. No browsing history is exposed. Everything runs locally on your network.

How It Works

1

Domain Queried

A DNS request hits your local resolver (dnsmasq)

2

AI Scores Risk

The local LLM analyzes the domain and returns a risk score

3

Adults Vote

Reviewers approve or reject the AI's assessment

4

Block or Allow

Permanent blocks only with high AI + human confidence
